Description:

This is a delicious vegan lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • ½ cup chopped parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 2 pounds firm tofu

  • ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
  • ¼ cup chopped parsley
  • ½ teaspoon salt
